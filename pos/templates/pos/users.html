Role Permissions

Super Admin

  • ✅ View ALL stores globally
  • ✅ Create & manage stores
  • ✅ Create & assign users
  • ✅ Set Area Manager PINs
  • ✅ View all reports & export
  • ❌ Cannot bill (admin only)

Store Owner

  • ✅ Full access in their store
  • ✅ Retail & wholesale billing
  • ✅ Inventory management
  • ✅ Reports + Excel export
  • ✅ Record expenses
  • ❌ Cannot manage other stores

Area Manager

  • ✅ Approves wholesale bills via PIN
  • ✅ Can be assigned to multiple stores
  • ✅ Has a secret 4–6 digit approval PIN
  • ✅ All approvals are logged with name
  • ❌ Cannot bill or access inventory
  • ❌ Cannot view reports

Staff

  • ✅ Retail & wholesale billing
  • ✅ View & restock inventory
  • ❌ Cannot view reports
  • ❌ Cannot delete products
  • ❌ Cannot manage users
